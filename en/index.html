About Our Work

Street cataloguing is an extremely slow process and it is necessary to physically cover an area in order to detect and list every point of interest within it.

Clearly, this costs a great amount of money and human resources.

Atlas Innovation intends to automate the entire process, which will immensely reduce the effort the current system requires.

Service

We catalogue any feature requested by any client.

For that purpose, we meet with our clients to discuss their necessities (which area is to be catalogued, what features are needed, etc.)

From that moment onwards, the company devolopers will assume the cataloguing process.

Ultimately, the client will be given a catalogue containing all the elements requested and their respective location which will have to be validated after the identification of possible false points of interest.

The new concept and its position in the industry

Today, the cataloging of a feature in an image is manually done by an interested party or a contractor.

This process requires a physical scan of the target area, which is both time and resource consuming.

Atlas Innovation’s aim is to automatically detect features and catalgue points of interest using image processing with reduced costs.
